## Funding process

<img src={Funding} alt="Funding" width="400" style={{ display: "block" }}/>
<small style={{ display: "block", marginBottom: "1rem"  }}></small>

There is a lot of way within the "***Get Funding***" process

Basically we plan to open doors to Funding once a very solid developing team with a solid action plan is ready to be reunited and put hands into the project

Funding will come in different stages, before the last big one to actually build The Orivon Browser

We plan to split the funding in 3 stages *(numbers are indicative and may change)*:

 - ***Initial test grant (30.000 - 80.000$):*** That initial small grant serves to prove that the Orivon team is able to keep up with promised goals and will finance the development of core essentials components of Orivon enough to offer the firsts functional MVP's proofs

 - ***Product core funding (150.000 - 400.000$)***: Our goal with that funding stage is to create an actually reliable enough product which is able to prove the Orivon value to users, test integration with some Apps and Web3 system which will prove the effectiveness of the Orivon model, an experimental alpha release to play with will be available

 - ***Build funding (750.000$ - 2.000.000$)***: This is considered the funding required to make the Orivon Browser become true, it includes a stable SDK for community devs and Integrations, stable browser release along with all of the promised features  

    It includes also the creation of the Orivon DAO

    Then, it's the most important part for the minds behind Orivon to effectively spread the browser to the masses (both to Web3, devs, and users) and we take responsibility to push it to the market in the most effective way
    

Additional fundings plans further these could be available to boost long term maintenance or to apply further projects milestones strategic to the Orivon interests

We have the interest into further innovation for Web3 after the Orivon Browser will be made up
