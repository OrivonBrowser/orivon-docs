# Get Involved

Getting involved into Orivon is not just contributing to a project, it's building the future of the Web3  

Becoming part of Orivon means having a chance to place you as one of the founding fathers who changed Web3 forever  
Means that you know Orivon leads to improve drastically the Web3 ecosystem and make it ready for the worldwide adoption  
And finally make Web3 a widespread technology

*The Orivon Project* is the main community movement which believes in the Orivon idea, here we explain how to get involved

We can build big things together

## How I can get involved

It does not matter what your skills currently are, there are very valuable roles which you can take part into Orivon, once you get involved efforts will be paid out with a fair amount of governance tokens into the [Orivon DAO](/docs/dao-plan) once it will be finally launched

Then, your first question will probably be: "Where do I begin?"

#### What roles I can get?

There are various figures that Orivon highly need  
Some hasn't any pre-requisite skill as you will learn as you go  
Others are specific skilled figures

Feel free to choose the one you like the most:

- **🟢 Community growth** *includes networking, creating articles, systems for community growth etc...*
- **🟢 Rust/C++ Developer** *technical backbone of the project*
- **HTML/CSS/JS Developer** *work along other Orivon devs to build the various UI*
- **NodeJS Developer** *build MVP's, example implementations and scripts*
- **Partnership** *we are always open to evaluate partnerships with other projects aligned with our values*
- **Collaborator** *If you are unsure of what role to take or it isn't listed here just come and talk in our [discord](https://discord.gg/DuRg87MvgD), we will figure it out*

We will also look for figures like:

- **Solidity Developer** *build the DAO rules based on Aragon or OZ Governance*
- **Investor** *funding and grants*

#### Where I can propose myself?

Once you joined our [**Discord**](https://discord.gg/DuRg87MvgD) hop into the ***# 📝 general*** channel
