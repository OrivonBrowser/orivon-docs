# Get Involved

Getting involved in Orivon is not just contributing to a project, it's helping build the future of Web3.

Becoming part of Orivon means having the chance to position yourself as one of the founding pioneers who helped change Web3 forever.  
It means believing that Orivon can drastically improve the Web3 ecosystem and make it ready for worldwide adoption.  
And ultimately, it means helping make Web3 a truly widespread technology.

*Orivon Foundation* is the core community movement that believes in the Orivon idea. Here we explain how you can get involved.

We can build great things together.

## How can I get involved?

It does not matter what your current skills are. There are many valuable roles you can take part in within Orivon. Once you get involved, your efforts will be rewarded with a fair amount of governance tokens in the [Orivon DAO](/docs/dao-plan) once it is finally launched.

Your first question will probably be: **"Where do I begin?"**

#### What roles can I take?

There are various roles that Orivon highly needs.  
Some do not require any prior skills, as you will learn along the way.  
Others require specific technical expertise.

Feel free to choose the one you like the most:

- **🟢 Community Growth** *includes networking, writing articles, building systems for community growth, etc.*
- **🟢 Rust/C++ Developer** *technical backbone of the project*
- **HTML/CSS/JS Developer** *work with other Orivon developers to build various UI components*
- **Node.js Developer** *build MVPs, example implementations, and scripts*
- **Partnerships** *we are always open to evaluating partnerships with other projects aligned with our values*
- **Collaborator** *if you are unsure which role to take or it is not listed here, just come talk to us in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/DuRg87MvgD) and we will figure it out together*

We will also be looking for roles such as:

- **Solidity Developer** *build the DAO rules based on Aragon or OpenZeppelin Governance*
- **Investor** *funding and grants*

#### Where can I apply?

Once you join our [**Discord**](https://discord.gg/DuRg87MvgD), hop into the ***# 📝 general*** channel.

<img src={DiscordChannel} alt="DiscordChannel" width="300"/>

Then type a message saying that you want to get involved in the project.

<img src={DiscordInvolvedMessage} alt="DiscordInvolvedMessage" width="400"/>

After that, one of our team members will contact you privately and guide you through the process of becoming part of Orivon.

You can also look up at our [**Internal Documentation**](https://orivonbrowser.github.io/internal-docs/docs/developer-work/) with guidance for Technical contributors on guidelines for contribution

We are truly grateful for your interest in getting involved. Every contribution is greatly appreciated ♥️
